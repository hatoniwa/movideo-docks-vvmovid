How to Play

• Choose one of the 5 image buttons.
• Listen to the 5 audio clips (A–E).
• Guess which voice matches the selected image.
• You get 50 points for a correct answer on the first try.
• The score decreases each time you miss.
• Try all 5 images to finish the game.

Good luck — trust your intuition!
